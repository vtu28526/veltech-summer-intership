A compact Flask and SQLite application for county-level disease outbreak risk prediction.

01

What it does

The system scores county-level outbreak risk, explains the strongest risk drivers, and saves predictions.

02

Scoring method

The score combines vaccination gap, social vulnerability, density, uninsured rate, chronic disease, poverty, housing, and environmental exposure indicators.
